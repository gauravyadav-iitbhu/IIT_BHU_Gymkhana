About

Theatre began under the name of “ABHIVYAKTI”, in which the marvels like Varun Grover, Vaibhav Suman, Sumit Saxena & Kumar Mayank used to enact epic Stage Plays which were put forth to audience along with Plays from other colleges. In 2013, the Theatrical society of IIT BHU was given a name, MASQUERADES under which various forms of Drama like Skits, Street Plays, Stage Plays, Mimes, etc. found existence. Coming to the achievements of the Club so far, Alumni like Varun Grover, Vaibhav Suman, Kumar Mayank, Sumit Saxena have set benchmarks of their utter creativity in the Bollywood Industry. Varun Grover as a writer, lyricist, actor, has done work in movies like “Masaan”, “Gangs of Wasseypur” & “Bombay Velvet” respectively. Vaibhav Suman & Sumit Saxena have written the dialogues of the movie “Pyaar ka Panchnama”. Kumar Mayank has acted in the movies like “Masaan” & “Praag”.
