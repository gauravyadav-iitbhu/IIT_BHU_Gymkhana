About

Quizzing is not about knowing every piece of trivia you can cram into your brain. It is about letting your imagination flower: first and foremost, quizzing is about lateral thinking. And then about your inquisitiveness about anything and everything. It is about the smarts you can spout intelligently wherever you go, because unfailingly, the best quizzers are supremely intelligent.
IIT BHU Quiz Club is one of the best quizzing hotspots in the entire country, currently ranked 5th among all IITs and IIMs. The club regularly wins laurels wherever they go: IIT Kanpur, MNNIT Allahabad's Gnosiomania, IIT-IIM Quizfest Nihilanth, IIT-Kharagpur's Springfest and a host of other competitions, not to forget TATA Crucible's Campus Quiz, where the club has been grabbing the top spot consecutively since the last 3 years. Even in the college, the Quiz Club is one of the most prolific and tightly integrated clubs which organises a plethora of events round the year: the Annual Quizzing Championship, Kashiyatra's Enquizta, Technex's Quizfest as well as quizzes in various department fests such as Chemical engineering's Udbhav and Ceramic engineering's Keramos. The club holds the record for making happen the biggest event the college has ever seen (apart from Prime Minister Modi's visit): Kashiyatra 2014's IMS India Quiz, which saw a record participation of 5700+ students. As such, the club has become a hotspot for the most creative and innovative minds of the college to share their ideas and forge bonds which last a lifetime.
