About

Animation Club IIT BHU, is a place for people who have a passion for digital art and video. Its an integral part of FMC and one of the most interesting clubs out there .Here we do three things , the first is to learn new things about animation and 3d modelling, our only resource being the window that is internet, secondly we make videos and teasers for our college and its various clubs and events. and all that we deliver is nothing short of pure visual nirvana . and thirdly and our most favourite thing we do here at the club is create something new, make something never before seen that origins right out of our minds and imagination, and unleash our inner Picasso. The Secretary of the club Aditya Solanki, is a expert at 3D modelling and rendering and has really good knowledge about professional softwares like Maya and 3DS Max . the Joint Secretary of the club Maitreya Dange works around the compositing software Adobe After Effects and loves to make Motion Graphics and VFX centric shorts. Nisha Choudhary , Joint Secretary of the club loves to do a bit of both Animation and 3D Modelling and is a really avid learner.
