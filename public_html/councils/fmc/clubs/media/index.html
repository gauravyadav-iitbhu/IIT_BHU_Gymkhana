About

THE MEDIA CLUB Introduction: Media Club : The ultimate value of life depends upon awareness rather than mere survival. Media, as we know, is the treasure trove of information.  We bring to you the latest news, awesome events, exclusive interviews and introspective articles. Our major publications are The Quest, the official institute newspaper and the Freshmen Survival Guide.  To provide instant updates we have a facebook page and are planning to launch an App very soon. The Media Club provides a platform for budding writers to hone their skills and gives them a platform to showcase their skills. We Organise Events such as writing competitions, RJ hunts to give the college an opportunity to experience and be part of the work we do.
