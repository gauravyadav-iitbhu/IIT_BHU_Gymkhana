About

No matter how sophisticated the camera, the photographer is still the one that makes the picture. We, the photographers of IIT (B.H.U.), immortalize memories. It’s no exaggeration when we say we are the eyes of this institute. A family of some of the most creative guys of our college, The Photography Club has a legacy of its own. We capture every activity, fest and event held in our institute. Apart from memorializing these festivities we have several events of our own including photowalks, VISTA (our annual photography competition) and several other online as well as offline competitions. We conduct several workshops to train freshers in the functioning of DSLRs, as well as teach them post- processing softwares such as Adobe Photoshop and Lightroom. We constantly learn from one another and better ourselves. There are several people associated with our club and we are growing in number with each passing year.
